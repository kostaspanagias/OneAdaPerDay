What is "One ADA Per Day"?

It's a global community initiative with one simple goal: to encourage every Cardano supporter, everywhere in the world, to buy just one ADA per day.

It's not about getting rich. It's about forming a consistent habit, showing daily support, and uniting the community under one positive, sustainable action that literally everyone can do.

Why This Simple Habit is So Powerful

Build an Atomic Habit

Small, daily actions create remarkable results. Buying one ADA isn't a financial burden, it's a powerful daily ritual. It builds consistency and compounds your personal stake in the network over time.

Unite the Community

Let's find common ground. Instead of drama, let's focus on a shared, positive goal. This simple act unifies us, amplifying our individual efforts into a collective voice of support.

Create Buying Pressure

Imagine 1,000, 10,000, or 100,000 of us buying every single day. This creates a steady, consistent, and organic demand for ADA, supporting the entire ecosystem from the ground up.

Accessible to Everyone

No excuses. Whether you're in a high or low-income country, one ADA is affordable. This is the most inclusive movement possible. Everyone can participate and make a difference.

Absolutely Sustainable

Because the commitment is so small, it's sustainable for the long term. Even if the price of ADA skyrockets, buying one per day will remain feasible for almost everyone. The habit continues.

Grow the Ecosystem

This movement is a story. A global community united in a daily action is a powerful, positive narrative. It generates publicity and shows the world the passion of the Cardano community.

How to Join the Movement

  1. 1.

    Set a Daily Reminder

    Use your phone, calendar, or an app. Set a recurring daily alarm. Consistency is the key.

  2. 2.

    Buy Your One ₳

    Log in to your favorite exchange or DEX and make your daily purchase. It only takes a minute.

  3. 3.

    Share the Habit

    Post on X (Twitter), Reddit, or your favorite social platform with the hashtag #OneAdaPerDay. Encourage others to join!

Pro Tip: Once a week or month, move your ADA from the exchange to your own self-custody wallet (like Eternl, Yoroi, or Lace) and stake it. Secure your keys, support the network!
